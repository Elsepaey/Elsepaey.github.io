NewsBuzz

a Flutter app that provide users with a dynamic stream of news articles, Users can explore news across different categories and search for specific topics.

Features:
-The app will organize news articles into categories such as politics, sports and more.
-Users can search for news articles using keywords. -Tapping on an article will open a detailed view with the full article content.
-Users can read the article within the app or open it in a browser.
