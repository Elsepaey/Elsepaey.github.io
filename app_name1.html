Chem Geek

a flutter application to help students and chemicians to solve chemical problems :

Here are the key features:

-the app will provide tools to solve chemical reactions, balance equations, and predict products..
-The app will use a Transformer-based artificial neural network to convert images of organic structures (such as chemical diagrams) into molecular formulas.structures to molecular formulas .
-The app will use a drawing machine learning model to generate visual representations of the chemical structures corresponding to the given formulas.machine learning model
-Users can search for any chemical compound by name or formula and The app will provide information such as molecular weight, boiling point,and other relevant properties.
-solve chemical issues such as molarity and molecular formula .
