ToDo

a flutter application that help users manage their tasks and to-do lists.

Here are the key features:

-Users can create, edit, and delete tasks.
-Each task will have a title, description, due date, and status (completed or pending).
-The app will display tasks in a list format.
-Tapping on a task will open a detailed view showing task information.
-Users can mark tasks as completed or update their details.
